San Francisco is the cultural, commercial and financial center of Northern California. San Francisco was founded on June 29, 1776, when colonists from Spain established Presidio of San Francisco at the Golden Gate and Mission San Francisco de Asis a few miles away, all named for St. Francis of Assisi. The California Gold Rush brought rapid growth, making it the largest city on the West Coast at the time.

Known for its cool summers, fog, steep rolling hills, eclectic mix of architecture, and landmarks, San Francisco is a popular tourist destination. Popular landmarks include the Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars, the former Alcatraz Gederal Penitentiary, Fisherman's harf, and it's Chinatown district. It is also the headquarters of many large companies suchs as Fitbit, Salesforce.com, Reddit, Yelp, Pinterest, Twitter, Uber and more.
