New York City is the most populous city in the United States with an estimated 2016 population of 8.5 million. The city's fast pace defines the term New York minute. New York is home to the headquarters of the United Nations. It is situated on one of the world's largest natural harbors and consists of five boroughs which is a separate county of New York.

There are many districts and landmarks in New York City and received a record 61 million tourists in 2016. The names of many of the city's bridges, skyscrapers and parks are know around the world. Ancorched by Wall Street in the Financial District of Lower Manhattan, New York City has been called both the most economically powerful city and the leading financial center of the world.
